The book

Biblical EQ is a 270 page book that covers topics such as:


  • The three types of emotions (holy, human and carnal).

  • Jesus as our emotional model, and the power of the Holy Spirit to help us emotionally

  • Our life perceptions and perspectives and how they influence our emotions.

  • Our beliefs, how they influence our emotions and how to change them for the better

  • How to manage strong emotions.


I have found that the book has been well-received by highly intelligent people such as Christian counselors, entrepreneurs, military officers, life-coaches, and leadership trainers. I have taught Biblical EQ in the USA, as well as in the Philippines and Malaysia.

- John Edmiston
(Author)
